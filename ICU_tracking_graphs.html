PathogenDB ICU Sample Tracking

Sampling Information

This page contains graphics that track the stool samples and rectal swabs being taken in the MICU, SICU and NSCU as part of the CDiff project.

All data is reported from the date of the first collection for that ICU.

The start dates for sample collection are:

  • NSIC: 11/17/2015
  • MICU: 12/1/2015
  • SICU: 9/14/2015
